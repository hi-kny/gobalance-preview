Good to know

Questions, answered

What does GoBalance track?
A sensor in the board reads every tilt and hold and turns it into a balance score that grades how well you moved — plus session history, streaks and program progress. It measures performance, not just play.
Do I need a subscription?
No subscription, ever. The free app includes unlimited Go Workout sessions — Balance, Plank, Boat and Bridge — plus a free trial of professional trainer video sessions. Structured programs are optional, one-time $4.99–$9.99 courses that last for 60 days. Buy only the ones you want.
How long is a session?
As long as you want. Do a quick reset, or follow a coach-led program for a full balance-day session. You set the pace.
What's in the box?
The 18-inch anti-slip wooden board with its built-in sensor, a removable TPE yoga layer, a pro balance cushion, and a pump.
Which devices work with it?
iOS 11+ and Android 5+ over Bluetooth. You can also cast your session to a supported TV.
What about returns?
Buy direct for a 30-day money-back guarantee and a 1-year warranty. On Amazon, standard Amazon returns apply.
